Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

At ACME MOTORS LLC, your privacy is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you browse our inventory, submit contact inquiries, or complete our secure auto finance pre-qualification credit applications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information from you when you interact with our dealership website, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and messaging preferences.
  • Credit Application Details: Social Security Number (SSN), date of birth, housing history (rent/own, address), employment details, and proof of income (when completing our financing pre-qualification form).
  • Vehicle Preferences: Vehicles of interest and estimation variables selected on our Buy-Here-Pay-Here (BHPH) calculator.

2. How We Use Your Information

We utilize the gathered details solely to serve your automotive purchasing needs, specifically to:

  • Evaluate financial options and credit eligibility for Buy-Here-Pay-Here (BHPH) or bank-financed automotive lending paths.
  • Deliver requested payment details and vehicle availability estimations.
  • Improve our dealership inventory, services, and online pre-qualification workflow.

3. Secure Storage & Encryption

We utilize industry-grade encryption and secure database structures (via Supabase with row-level security and active TLS) to prevent unauthorized access or interception of sensitive data, including credit and income verification applications. No unauthorized scripts or tracking cookies are allowed to capture data from our submission channels.

4. Third-Party Sharing Rules

We do not sell, rent, or distribute your personal or financial data to outside marketing or advertising agencies. Your credit information is only shared with trusted financial partners and credit reporting institutions under strict security covenants, and only for the explicit purpose of processing your authorized bank financing applications.

5. Your Controls & Consent

By submitting a credit pre-qualification request, you authorize ACME MOTORS LLC to review your credit attributes. You have the right to request a full review, correction, or permanent removal of your contact details or application data from our systems by contacting us directly at info@example.com.
